Family Room

A family room is an informal, all-purpose room in a house. The family room is designed to be a place where family and guests gather for group recreation like talking, reading, watching TV, and other family activities. ... The term family room is defined in the 1945 book Tomorrow's House by George Nelson and Henry Wright. Good Design

Good design is honest. It does not make a product more innovative, powerful or valuable than it really is. It does not attempt to manipulate the consumer with promises that cannot be kept. Good design is long-lasting. ... Care and accuracy in the design process show respect towards the user.Good design is not just what looks good. It also needs to perform, convert, astonish, and fulfill its purpose.
